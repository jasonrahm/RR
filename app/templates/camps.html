2018 FLL Summer Camps!

Each summer, the Robo Raiders teach week-long day-camps for kids ages 8 through 14, with a three-day option as well for K-3rd students.

During these camps, we use NXT Lego robot kits to teach kids the basics of NXT programming, core values, problem solving, and provide them with their own game challenge to complete and showcase at the end of the week.

In our advanced camp, we provide an opportunity for kids with experience in FLL programming to learn advanced EV3 programming and test their problem-solving skills with a modified FLL game challenge.

For both weeks of camp, we offer morning (9-11:45) and afternoon (1-3:45) sessions.
