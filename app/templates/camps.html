2019 FLL Summer Camps!

Each summer, the Robo Raiders teach week-long day-camps for kids ages 8 through 14, with a three-day option as well for K-3rd students. During these camps, we use NXT Lego robot kits to teach kids the basics of NXT programming, core values, problem solving, and provide them with their own game challenge to complete and showcase at the end of the week.

In our advanced camp, we provide an opportunity for kids with experience in FLL programming to learn advanced EV3 programming and test their problem-solving skills with a modified FLL game challenge. We had a fantastic two weeks of summer camp this year and we were so proud of what the students were able to accomplish! We look forward to doing it again next year, so keep a look out for updates!
