2019 FLL Summer Camps!

Each summer, the Robo Raiders teach half day, week-long camps for students entering 2nd-8th grades. During these camps, we use Lego robot kits to teach the basics of programming, core values, problem solving, and provide students with a FIRST Robotics themed challenge to complete and showcase at the end of the week.

Intro to Robotics is for students entering 2nd-4th grades and uses the WeDo platform. It will feature the Moon Mission challenge from FIRST LEGO League Junior.

Robotics I is for students entering 4th-8th grades who have no previous robotics experience. Using the NXT LEGO robot platform, they will learn basics of drop and drag programming, sensor use, and finish off the week with the Into Orbit themed challenge from FIRST LEGO League.

In our Robotics II Advanced camp, we provide an opportunity for kids with experience in FLL programming to learn advanced EV3 programming, build more complicated attachments, and test their problem-solving skills with the Into Orbit game challenge.
